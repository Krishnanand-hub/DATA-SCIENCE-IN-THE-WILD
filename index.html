The Model

Risk Score = 0.4 × Rrepos + 0.3 × Rhpi + 0.3 × Rdeprivation
Rrepos — Normalised repossession rate (higher repos = higher risk)
Rhpi — Inverted HPI momentum (falling prices = higher risk)
Rdeprivation — IMD deprivation score (higher deprivation = higher risk)
Capital Allocation ∝ (1 − Risk Score) / Σ(1 − Risk Scores)
Expected Return — Regions with lower risk receive proportionally more capital
